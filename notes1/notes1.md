### Notes from week 1

1. *What is markdown?*

2. *What is Linux?*
   
   A ***Unix-like OS*** that is open source, highly customizable and compatible, used in the vast majority of server applications

3. *What is Ubuntu?*
   
   One of the most wellknown ***Linux Distros*** used by both community and professionals
